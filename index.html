Magic The Gathering

Magic The Gathering is a trading card game made by the company Wizards Of The Coast. Each new set of cards represent a different plane or world and is named after said plane.
The latest released set is called Innistrad: Crimson Vow and is about a vampire wedding on the plane of Innistrad.

You can use the cards to battle opponents in fun filled matches or you can just simply try to collect them all. The cards feature stunningly beautiful artwork wich makes collecting them very rewarding.

There's also an online version of the game which lets you play with millions of users over the entire world.
